ITU Hydros Team

I worked as an electronics responsible for ITU Hydros team.

Our project was carried out with the aim of enabling a boat designed as an unmanned boat to navigate efficiently as a result of cutting its contact with water by means of hydrofoils.

We had the opportunity to compete our project in the Switzerland-based 2018 HydroContest competition, which was held for the fifth time in Saint Tropez, France.
